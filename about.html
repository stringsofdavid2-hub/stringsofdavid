About Me

Hello! I am Samuel, a passionate musician and dedicated teacher. I have been guiding students in Keyboard, Piano, Guitar, Ukulele, Drums, and Vocal training for several years.

My mission is to help every student discover their musical talent and joy, while building confidence and creativity through music.

Our Academy

Strings Of David Music Academy focuses on nurturing musical talent of all ages. We combine strong fundamentals, practical training, and live performance opportunities to create a fun and professional learning environment.

Every student receives personal attention and structured lessons to grow their skills and love for music.
